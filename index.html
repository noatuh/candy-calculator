Reese's Miniatures!

Reese's Miniatures are small bite-sized chocolates that consist of a peanut butter filling coated with a layer of milk chocolate. They are made by The Hershey Company and are a popular candy in the United States. Reese's Miniatures are similar in taste and texture to the larger Reese's Peanut Butter Cups, but are smaller in size and packaged in a variety of fun and festive wrappers. They are often sold in bags or containers and are a favorite treat for many people who enjoy the combination of peanut butter and chocolate.
